Chong Xiang    

I am a PhD student at Princeton University advised by Prof. Prateek Mittal. I am currently working on the intersection of Security, Privacy, and Machine Learning.

I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) with a Bachelor's degree in 2019. I was a member of Network Security and Privacy Protection (NSEC) Lab and was advised by Prof. Haojin Zhu.

Please feel free to reach out via cxiang@printceton.edu if you have questions on my research, are looking for collaboration opportunities, etc.
