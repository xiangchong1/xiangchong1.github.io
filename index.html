Chong Xiang

I am a PhD student at Princeton University, advised by Prof. Prateek Mittal. I am interested in identifying and fixing Security and Privacy vulnerabilities of Machine Learning systems.

I have developed a series of certifiably robust defenses against adversarial patch attacks, including PatchGuard, PatchGuard++, PatchCleanser, PatchCURE, DetectorGuard, and ObjectSeeker. Here is a 35-min tutorial on this research field (recorded in April 2023).

I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) with a Bachelor's degree in 2019. I was a member of Network Security and Privacy Protection (NSEC) Lab and was advised by Prof. Haojin Zhu (2016-2019). I also worked with Prof. Bo Li as a research intern at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (2018).

Here is my [Curriculum Vitae]. Please feel free to reach out via email at cxiang@princeton.edu :)
